Proposition 3.3.3. A relatively prime Euclid’s Lemma.

If \(m,n, k \in \Z\) are positive integers and \(m\) and \(n\) are relatively prime, such that \(m\) divides \(kn\text{,}\) then \(m\) divides \(k\text{.}\)
