Proposition 3.3.4. If relatively prime integers divide, their product divides.

If \(m,n, c \in \Z\) are integers greater than 1 and \(m\) and \(n\) are relatively prime, such that \(m\) divides \(c\) and \(n\) divides \(c\text{,}\) then \(mn\) divides \(c\text{.}\)
