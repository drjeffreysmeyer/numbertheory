Proposition 1.2.7. The minimal positive element is 1.

If \(R\) is a ordered ring whose positive elements are well-ordered, then the minimal positive element is 1.
🔗
🔗

Hint.

Try a proof by contradiction. Let \(e\) denote the minimal positive element of \(R\) and suppose \(0 < e < 1\text{.}\) What happens when you multiply by \(e\text{?}\) Can you find a contradiction?
