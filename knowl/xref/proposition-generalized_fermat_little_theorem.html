Proposition 4.1.2. Generalized Fermat’s Little Theorem.

If \(a, m \in \Z\text{,}\) \(m > 1\) is a positive integer, such that \(\gcd(a,m)=1\text{,}\) then \(a^{\phi(m)} \equiv 1 \pmod{m}\text{.}\)
