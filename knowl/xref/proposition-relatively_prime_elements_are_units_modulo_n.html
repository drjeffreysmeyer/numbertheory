Proposition 3.4.2. Relatively Prime Elements are Units Modulo m.

If \(m > 1\) is an integer, and \(a\) is a least residue modulo \(m\) such that \(\gcd(a,m)=1\text{,}\) then \(a \in U_m\text{.}\)
